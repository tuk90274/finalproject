Today, all of Slipknot's music videos are directed by their percussionist Shawn Crahan. Some videos parodied horror movies, others were symbolic and had their own terrifying theme to it. A few older videos show the band performing on stage. The music video for "Spit It Out" parodies the iconic horror movie The Shining. "Wait and Bleed" and "The Nameless" feature a live performance of the band. "Psychosocial" was shot on a field and featured large "purgatory masks" of each member to symbolize the band's ego. Additionally, all music videos of the band surpass millions of views.
